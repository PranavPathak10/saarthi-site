Saarthi, as the name suggests is an organization that aims to be the guiding light in the lives of students, young professionals and anyone who has the zeal and passion for continuous learning. Saarthi has the curriculum and courses for school, college students in both technical and non-technical streams. Along with that, Saarthi also caters to youngsters, who are seeking jobs in various domains. Software Development, Soft skills, Public speaking, Leadership and many more essential employability skills are the key training areas at Saarthi.
