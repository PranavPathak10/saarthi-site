About Saarthi

Saarthi, as the name suggests, is an organization that aims to be the guiding light in the lives of students, young professionals, and anyone who has the zeal and passion for continuous learning.

We provide curriculum and courses for school and college students in both technical and non-technical streams. Saarthi also helps young job seekers prepare with essential employability training including Software Development, Soft Skills, Public Speaking, and Leadership.
